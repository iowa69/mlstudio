Welcome

  1. Enter a folder containing .fasta assemblies (optionally with matching paired-end _R1_/_R2_ FASTQs).
  2. Pick a scheme.
  3. Click Analyze.
  4. Upload an optional metadata CSV and pick a field to color by.

Tip: drag nodes to lay them out manually. Drag with right mouse button to pan. Scroll to zoom.
