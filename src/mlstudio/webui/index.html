Welcome

  1. Enter a folder containing .fasta assemblies (optionally with paired-end _R1_/_R2_ FASTQs).
  2. Pick an MLST or cgMLST scheme — or pull a new one from the catalog.
  3. Click Analyze.
  4. Upload a metadata CSV to color the tree by any field. Adjust the cluster halo distance to highlight outbreak clusters.

Tip · drag nodes to re-arrange. Right-drag to pan. Scroll to zoom.
